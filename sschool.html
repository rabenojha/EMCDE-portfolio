FOSS4G and SoTM

FOSS4G and SoTM 2022

I first came to know about the Free and Open Source for Geospatial (FOSS4G) conference when I was doing my undergrad back in Nepal. So, I have been following it for a few years now. As I moved to Europe last year to attend graduate school and FOSS4G 2022 and State of the Map 2022 were happening in Italy, I was really looking forward to both conferences.

State of the Map 2022

Date: August 19, 2022 - August 21, 2022

Venue: Florence, Italy


State of the Map is the yearly event that brings OpenStreetMap (OSM) enthusiasts from all over the world to one roof. This year in Florence, there were 407 people on site and 193 online attending the conference.

The 3 days were full of talks, workshops, posters, academic tracks related to OSM mapping, new tools and technologies developed around OSM, research and findings based on the data, works of the local communities in their regions, etc. And not to forget, the social interactions where I could meet many new people and catch up with the old ones.


Learn more about the State of the Map here.



FOSS4G 2022

Date: August 22, 2022 - August 28, 2022

Venue: Florence, Italy


Workshops

The first 2 days of the conference were full of workshops. As the workshops ran in parallel and were several hours long, I could only attend few.

Manage your geospatial data with PostgreSQL/PostGIS:

Learnt the basic introduction to SQL and PostgreSQL, the possibilities of using SQL instead of some Desktop GIS’s functionalities, storing and using geospatial data in databases, etc.The full material of the workshop can be accessed here.

Developing custom GRASS tools:

GRASS GIS, was a tool that I heard a lot about but never used before. In this workshop, the trainers showed us how to build on the Python scripts to GRASS tools, best practices in doing so, building Graphical User Interface (GUI) and Command Line Interface (CLI), and publishing it to the central repository. The full material of the workshop can be accessed here.
