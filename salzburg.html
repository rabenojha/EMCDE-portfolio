Geohumanitarian Action

Geohumanitarian Action

There has been ongoing violence in Cabo Delgado, northern province of Mozambique since late 2017.

For this seminar course, I prepared a paper on the cause of the violence in Cabo Delgado and the social and economic impacts it has on people and locale.The main focus is on different geospatial and earth observation tools, and see how they can be and are being used in humanitarian assistance.

This website is prepared to showcase my works during the Erasmus Mundus Copernicus Master in Digital Earth.
