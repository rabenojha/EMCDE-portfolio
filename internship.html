Internship

As part of the curriculum from the university for my Master studies, I did 2 months long internship at the German Aerospace Center (DLR).
The German Remote Sensing Data Center (DFD) falls under the Earth Observation Center (EOC) at the DLR and consists of several departments.
I was part of the Agricultural and Forest Ecosystems team and AGRAIN project under the Department of Land Surface Dynamics. My works were supervised by Dr. Andreas Hirner, senior scientist at the same department.

The internship was held at the DLR facility in Operffafenhofen near Munich, Germany.

Date: July - August, 2022
Position: Remote Sensing Analyst Intern

EOC office at DLR, Munich

The following activities were carried out by me during the internship period:

  • Analysis of European Centre for Medium-Range Weather Forecasts (ECMWF) data from the Climate Data Store (CDS) as input dataset for the Biosphere Energy Transfer Hydrology (BETHY) model to determine the Net Primary Production (NPP) in West Africa.
  • Implementation of download routines using Jupyter Notebooks.
  • Implementation of conversion routines of the downloaded time series to BETHY compatible input data format in Python using Xarray library and Jupyter Notebook.
  • Generation of executable Python programs for operational use.
  • Validation and documentation of the results.
